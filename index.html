About me

Air Force 1 sneakers are:

The Air Force 1 sneaker, released in 1982, is a product of Nike Inc. It was created by designer Bruce Kilgore, and was the first basketball sneaker released by Nike to use Nike Air technology.

The Nike Air force 1 (abbreviated AF1) has had the same similar style for 25 years with minimal changes. The name is a reference to the Air Force One aircraft, which is responsible for transporting the President of the United States of America.
